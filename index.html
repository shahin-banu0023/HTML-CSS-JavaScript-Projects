Sun Star Moon

The Sun, Moon, and stars are fundamental celestial bodies: the Sun is our solar system's star, a giant plasma ball providing light and heat via nuclear fusion, vital for life. The Moon is Earth's natural satellite, orbiting us and reflecting sunlight, causing tides and phases. Stars are distant, luminous suns, appearing as twinkling points, with constellations being patterns of them, while our Sun is just one average star in the vast universe.
